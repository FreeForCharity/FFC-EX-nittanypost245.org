## Email Body

**Bottom Line Up Front:** The March 19, 2026 Post 245 meeting approved key funding decisions, welcomed 1 new member and 6 transfers, authorized a PA Lottery six-month trial at the LITW, and confirmed our Spring cleanup day on April 18. Membership stands at 641 — 94.4% of our 679 goal.

---

Comrades,

Here is a quick recap of the March 19, 2026 stated meeting. Full minutes are available online and as a PDF download below.

**Key Decisions & Highlights:**

- 💰 **Finance:** Post balance is **$32,348.08** as of March 19. Savings account holds $10,112.17.
- 📊 **Membership:** We are at **641 members — 94.4% of our goal of 679.** Only 38 to go!
- 🎰 **PA Lottery approved (6-month trial):** Keno, Horse Racing, and Lottery ticket machines will be added to the LITW at no cost to us; Post earns 5% of sales. HA board already approved.
- 🌱 **Spring Cleanup Day: April 18, 2026**, 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM. Budget of $3,742 approved.
- 🇺🇸 **Vietnam Veterans Brunch:** Free brunch for Vietnam Veterans on **March 29**, 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM.
- 👤 **New Member accepted:** Olivia Thornton (Global War / USA) — passed 17-0.
- 🔄 **6 Transfers accepted** (Robert J. Levitt, Jacob D. Reddington, Dennis E. Johnsonbaugh, Louis A. Berrena, Brian L. Doyon, James M. Stuhltrager) — passed 17-0.
- 💲 **Member dues support:** Chuck Brickell donated $2,000 + Post approved $1,288 to cover Over 80, Military & Hardship member dues for 2027.
- 📧 **New Post email:** info@nittanypost245.org is live. The old address will auto-forward starting in June.
- 🏚️ **Post memorials:** We remember Bradley A. Fisher and John Brickell.

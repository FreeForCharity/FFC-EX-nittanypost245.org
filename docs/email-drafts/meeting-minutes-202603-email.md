## BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)

Key decisions and highlights from the March 19, 2026 meeting:

- **Finances are strong:** Post balance is $32,348.08 with a savings account balance of $10,112.17.
- **Membership at 94.4% of goal:** We stand at 641 members — only 38 away from our goal of 679. Keep spreading the word!
- **PA Lottery approved for the LITW:** The Post voted to allow a six-month trial of Keno, Horse Racing, and lottery machines at the Legion in the Woods. The Lottery provides monitors and internet at no cost; LITW earns 5% of sales.
- **Spring Cleanup Day is April 18, 2026** (8:00 AM – 4:00 PM). Please plan to help out!
- **Vietnam Veterans Free Brunch – March 29, 2026** (11:00 AM – 1:00 PM). Share this with any Vietnam Veterans you know — all are welcome.
